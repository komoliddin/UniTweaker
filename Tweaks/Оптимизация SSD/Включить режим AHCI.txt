EN_TITLE:
Enable AHCI Mode

EN_DESCRIPTION:
AHCI (Advanced Host Controller Interface) is a mechanism used to connect hard drives via the SATA protocol, allowing for advanced features such as hot swapping and Native Command Queuing (NCQ). After applying this tweak, you must enable AHCI mode in your BIOS/UEFI settings before the next boot.
