CHECKBOX:
ROOT=HKLM
KEY=SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows NT\CurrentVersion\Multimedia\SystemProfile
NAME=MaxTimerResolution
TYPE=REG_DWORD
VALUE=1

CHECKED:
bcdedit /deletevalue useplatformclock
bcdedit /deletevalue tscsyncpolicy
bcdedit /set disabledynamictick yes

UNCHECKED:
bcdedit /set useplatformclock true
bcdedit /set tscsyncpolicy Enhanced
bcdedit /set disabledynamictick no

DESCRIPTION:
Отключает HPET (High Precision Event Timer), что снижает микрофризы в играх и задержку ввода.

EN_TITLE:
High Precision Event Timer (HPET)

EN_DESCRIPTION:
Disables HPET to reduce micro-stutters in games, improve control responsiveness, and lower input lag.

UZ_TITLE:

DE_TITLE:

DE_DESCRIPTION:
