CHECKBOX:
ROOT=HKCU
KEY=Software\Microsoft\Clipboard
NAME=EnableClipboardHistory
TYPE=REG_DWORD
VALUE=1

CHECKED:
reg add "HKCU\Software\Microsoft\Clipboard" /v EnableClipboardHistory /t REG_DWORD /d 1 /f
reg add "HKLM\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Windows\System" /v AllowClipboardHistory /t REG_DWORD /d 1 /f
net stop cbdhsvc
net start cbdhsvc
gpupdate /force

UNCHECKED:
reg add "HKCU\Software\Microsoft\Clipboard" /v EnableClipboardHistory /t REG_DWORD /d 0 /f
reg add "HKLM\SOFTWARE\Policies\Microsoft\Windows\System" /v AllowClipboardHistory /t REG_DWORD /d 0 /f
net stop cbdhsvc
net start cbdhsvc
gpupdate /force

DESCRIPTION:
Включает историю буфера обмена (Win+V).
